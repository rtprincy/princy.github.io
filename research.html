Research Overview

My research focuses on understanding the formation and evolution of hot sub-luminous stars. By leveraging multiband time-series data and employing advanced machine learning techniques, I aim to uncover the underlying processes that govern these intriguing celestial objects. This work involves analyzing extensive datasets, developing predictive models, and collaborating with a network of researchers to interpret the results.
